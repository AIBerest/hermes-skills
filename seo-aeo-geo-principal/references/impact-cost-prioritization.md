# Impact / effort prioritization

Priority formula:

```text
priority = (10 - score) × impact × confidence ÷ effort
```

Fix order:

1. Crawl/index blockers.
2. Metadata/H1 and sitemap gaps.
3. Structured data and entity graph.
4. Thin content and direct-answer gaps.
5. Agent-readable discovery.
6. Performance only when real measurement evidence exists.
