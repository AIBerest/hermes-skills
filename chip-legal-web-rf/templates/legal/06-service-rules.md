# Service/community rules template

These rules apply to access to `[PRODUCT_OR_COMMUNITY_NAME]`.

## User obligations

Users must:

- respect other users and moderators;
- not publish illegal, abusive, spam, or infringing content;
- not share paid access credentials or materials unless allowed;
- not disrupt the service or attempt unauthorized access.

## Access limits

The Operator may restrict or terminate access for material rule violations, fraud, abuse, payment disputes, or legal/security reasons.

## Moderation

Moderation decisions may include warning, content removal, temporary restriction, or permanent access termination.

Recommended checkbox:

```text
I accept the Service Rules and understand that access may be restricted for material violations.
```
