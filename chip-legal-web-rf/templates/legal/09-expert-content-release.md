# Expert / guest content release template

Use before recording interviews, workshops, webinars, podcasts, or guest materials.

I, `[EXPERT_NAME_OR_PLACEHOLDER]`, consent to photo/video/audio recording of my participation in `[EVENT_OR_CONTENT_NAME]` and to editing, reproduction, publication, distribution, and other lawful use of the resulting materials by `[OPERATOR_LEGAL_NAME]` for `[PURPOSES]`.

Territory: `[TERRITORY]`
Term: `[TERM]`
Compensation: `[PAID_OR_UNPAID]`
Contact: `[CONTACT]`
Date: `[DATE]`

Evidence to store:

- name or public identity;
- contact/source account;
- date/time of consent;
- event/content title;
- screenshot, signed file, email, or form submission.
