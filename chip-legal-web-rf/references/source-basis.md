# Source basis and legal caveat

This pack is built around common Russian website compliance patterns. It intentionally avoids private customer data and does not include full statutory analysis.

## Common source anchors to verify before production

- Federal Law No. 152-FZ “On Personal Data”, especially consent quality and withdrawal logic.
- Federal Law No. 38-FZ “On Advertising”, especially separate prior consent for advertising messages.
- Federal Law No. 126-FZ “On Communications”, when SMS/calls/messenger marketing is used.
- Law No. 2300-1 “On Protection of Consumer Rights”, when B2C paid services or distance sales are involved.
- Civil Code rules on offer/acceptance and paid services.
- Federal Law No. 54-FZ, if payment/fiscal receipt/KKT mechanics matter.

## Production rule

Before publishing a real pack, check the latest official text and the product-specific facts:

- legal entity/tax status;
- actual service description;
- payment provider and fiscalization route;
- refund/cancellation mechanics;
- personal-data categories and processors;
- analytics/cookies actually used;
- messaging channels and unsubscribe routes.
