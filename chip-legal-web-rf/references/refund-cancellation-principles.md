# Refund and cancellation principles

A generic offer should not use a blanket “no refunds” clause for every situation.

## Safer structure

- State when the service is considered started or delivered.
- Separate digital access, live services, consultations, subscriptions, and downloadable materials.
- Explain cancellation route and support contact.
- For recurring charges, explain how to stop future charges.
- Keep refund logic consistent with consumer-rights rules and actual product mechanics.
- Do not promise instant automatic refunds if the payment provider/manual process cannot support it.

## Product-owner facts required

- What exactly is delivered?
- When does access open?
- Can access be revoked automatically?
- Is there a trial?
- Is there a recurring charge?
- Are there non-refundable third-party costs?
- What support channel handles cancellation/refund requests?
